**Get your Microsoft volume (OneDrive + Outlook):**

👉 **[View OneDrive storage](https://onedrive.live.com/?v=managestorage)**
- Or via web: Gear icon (Settings) > OneDrive Settings > More Settings > Storage Metrics.
- Or via app: OneDrive icon > Settings > Manage Storage.

👉 **[View Outlook storage](https://outlook.live.com/mail/0/options/general/storage)**

Add OneDrive and Outlook values to get your total Microsoft volume.
