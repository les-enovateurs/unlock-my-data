1. **Delete posts (Photos, Carousels, Reels)**
   - Go to your profile and identify old videos (Reels) or long carousels.
   - Open the post, tap the three dots (•••) and select **Delete**.

2. **Manage your Archives (Stories and deleted posts)**
   - Go to your profile, tap the three lines (☰) in the top right > **Your activity**.
   - Go to **Recently deleted**. Items stay here for 30 days, but you can permanently delete them right away to free up data.
   - Go to **Archived**. You can permanently delete old automatically saved Stories from here.

3. **Clean up your Direct Messages (DM)**
   - Go to your inbox.
   - Delete long conversations containing many photos, videos or voice notes with accounts you no longer interact with.
