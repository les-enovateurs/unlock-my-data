1. **Sort out iCloud Drive**
   - On iOS/iPadOS: Go to **Files** > **Browse** > **iCloud Drive**.
   - Or on Mac/PC: Log in to [icloud.com](https://www.icloud.com/iclouddrive).
   - Sort items by size to find large unnecessary documents and delete them. Don't forget to empty the "Recently Deleted" folder.

2. **Clean up iCloud Photos**
   - Open the **Photos** app.
   - Go to **Albums** > **Media Types** (Videos, Screenshots, Duplicates).
   - Videos take up the most space, start there.
   - You MUST empty the **Recently Deleted** album to get the storage back immediately.

3. **Manage iPhone/iPad Backups**
   - On your iPhone: **Settings** > [Your Name] > **iCloud** > **Manage Account Storage** > **Backups**.
   - Delete backups of old devices you no longer own.
   - Disable cloud backups for apps that don't need them.
