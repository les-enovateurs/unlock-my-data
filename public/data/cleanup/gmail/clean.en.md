### Clean our inbox (Gmail)

1. **Delete large messages**
   - In the Gmail search bar, type `has:attachment larger:10M` (or `size:10m`) to filter emails larger than 10 MB.
   - Select old drafts and emails with unnecessary attachments and delete them.
2. **Clean up Spams**
   - Go to the "Spam" folder on the left side and click on "Delete all spam messages" at the top of the page.
3. **Empty the trash**
   - **Important** : deleted messages remain in the trash for 30 days and still take up space on your Google Quota.
   - In the left menu, go to "Trash" and click on **"Empty Trash"**.
