1. **Delete old heavy photos and videos**
   - Go to your profile then navigate to **Photos** > **Albums**.
   - Identify albums containing many videos or photos you no longer need and delete them.
   - Videos take up the most space: go to **Videos** and sort them out.

2. **Sort out your Activity Log**
   - Go to **Settings & Privacy** > **Activity Log**.
   - From here, you can bulk manage your past posts, interactions, and permanently empty items in your "Trash".

3. **Manage connected apps**
   - Go to **Settings** > **Apps and Websites**.
   - Remove access for games and apps you no longer use to limit your data sharing.
