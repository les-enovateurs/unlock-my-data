### Clean Gmail
1. Open [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/).
2. In the top search bar, type `has:attachment larger:5M` and press Enter.
3. This search shows all emails containing attachments larger than 5 MB.
4. **Check the box** at the top left to select them all, then click the **Trash** icon.
5. Don't forget to empty the trash!

### Clean Google Drive
1. Open [Google Drive Storage Quota](https://drive.google.com/drive/u/0/quota).
2. Your files will be listed from heaviest to lightest.
3. Select the ones you no longer need and delete them.
