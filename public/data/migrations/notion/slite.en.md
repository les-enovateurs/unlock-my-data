## How to Export a Single Page from Notion
1. Open the page in Notion.
2. Click the … menu in the top right and choose **Export**.
3. Use the following settings:
	- Export format: **HTML**
	- Include databases: **Default View**
	- Include content: **Everything**
	- Include subpages: **On**
	- Create folders for subpages: **On**
	- Export Comments: **Off**
4. Click **Export** and wait for the archive to download.

## How to Export a Notion Workspace
1. Click the name of your workspace in the top-left corner and choose **Settings**.
2. Go to **Workspace > General**.
3. In the Export section, click **Export** next to Workspace content.
4. Use the following settings:
	- Export format: **HTML**
	- Include databases: **Default view**
	- Include content: **Everything**
	- Include subpages: **On**
	- Create folders for subpages: **On**
	- Export Comments: **Off**
5. Click **Export** and wait for the archive to download.

## How to Import Your Content Into Slite
1. Click **Import** in the lower section of the left sidebar.
2. Select the **Notion** option.
3. Choose **Import one or more docs** or **Import full Notion workspace** and follow the on-screen instructions.
4. Choose a name for the new channel in which your doc(s) will be imported.
5. Choose the visibility of your new channel:
	- **Open**: Everyone in your workspace can access this channel.
	- **Private**: Only people you choose can access this channel.
6. Once the import is successful, you’ll see a confirmation dialog in the lower right corner. Click **Go to the doc** to view your imported doc(s).
7. If you get an error saying that you couldn't create the channel, contact your workspace administrator and ask them to grant you the necessary permissions to create new channels.

Learn more: [Slite Import Guide](https://slite.slite.page/p/KYFLSp6oBKT0jW/Importing-from-Notion)

## What Gets Imported
- The doc structure (what you see in your sidebar in Notion)
- The doc content, including:
  - Headings, bullet points, numbered lists, etc.
  - Hint blocks
  - Links between docs
  - Images
  - Videos
- Databases will be converted into Collections in Slite

Learn more: [What gets imported](https://slite.slite.page/p/8bd4HVVH-4KoaC)
