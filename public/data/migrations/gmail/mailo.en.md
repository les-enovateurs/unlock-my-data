## Before you start
- Check your Gmail storage (if >1 GB, clean up or upgrade your Mailo plan).
- Mailo imports all folders/labels except spam and unclassified archived messages.
- Messages with multiple labels will be imported multiple times.
- After transfer, keep your Gmail account active for at least a year to ensure new messages are collected.

## Step 1: Generate a Gmail app password
1. Enable two-step verification on your Google account.
2. Generate a 16-character app password for Mailo.
3. Copy this password; you’ll use it to authenticate on Mailo.

## Step 2: Start the transfer on Mailo
1. On Mailo web, go to Options (gear icon) → External account transfer.
2. Click "Transfer another account".
3. Enter your Gmail address and validate.
4. Paste the app password and validate.
5. Leave the options checked and validate to start the transfer.
6. Wait for the transfer to finish (Mailo will notify you).

## Automatic collection of future messages
- If enabled, Mailo will regularly fetch new Gmail messages.
- If there are issues, prefer Gmail’s automatic forwarding to Mailo.

## Import contacts and calendar
- Use the external account transfer tool (Options) to import contacts and calendar.
- See Mailo’s FAQ for other methods.
