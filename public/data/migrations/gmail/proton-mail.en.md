## Steps to enable Gmail → Proton Mail forwarding
1. In Proton Mail web: `Settings` → `All settings` → `Import via Easy Switch`.
2. Click "Set up auto-forwarding from Gmail".
3. Sign in to your Google/Gmail account and grant Proton the requested permissions.
4. Confirm the permissions.
5. Proton Mail will confirm that forwarding is enabled.

## How to disable automatic forwarding
1. Go back to `Import via Easy Switch` in Proton Mail.
2. In the "History" section, click "Remove" next to the forwarding you want to stop.

## Key points
- Forwarded messages are encrypted on arrival in Proton Mail.
- Spam is generally not forwarded.
- Deleting or archiving a message in Proton Mail does not affect Gmail.
- You can filter or organize forwarded emails in Proton Mail.
