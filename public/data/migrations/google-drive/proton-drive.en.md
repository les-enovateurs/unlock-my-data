1. **Export your Google Drive files**
    - Go to https://takeout.google.com and sign in to your Google Account.
    - Scroll to "Create a new export" and click **Deselect all**.
    - Find **Drive** in the list and check the box next to it.
    - Scroll to the bottom and select **Next step**.
    - Choose your transfer destination, export frequency, file type, and size. Click **Create export**.
    - Wait for the export to be prepared. You’ll receive a download link by email when it’s ready.

2. **Download your export**
    - Open the "Your Google data is ready to download" email and click **Manage Google Takeout request**.
    - Download all parts of the export.
    - Unzip the files on your computer.
    - *Tip: Proton Drive keeps the folder structure of uploaded files. If your export is split into multiple parts, you may want to reorganize or merge folders before uploading.*

3. **Import your files into Proton Drive**
    - **Desktop app (Windows, macOS):**
        - Install the Proton Drive app. It creates a dedicated folder on your computer.
        - Drag and drop your unzipped files into the Proton Drive folder. They will be uploaded automatically.
        - Check the sync status in the app to confirm upload completion.
    - **Web app:**
        - Go to https://drive.proton.me/ and sign in to your Proton Account.
        - (Optional) Create a new folder for your Google Drive files.
        - Upload your files by dragging them into Proton Drive or using the Upload (⬆️) button.
        - Monitor upload status in the web app.

For more details, see the official Proton Drive guide: https://proton.me/support/import-files-google-drive
