**TuneMyMusic**

Works with: Spotify, TIDAL, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, and many more.

1. Click "Start transfer" to sign in or create a Deezer account
2. Select your streaming service
3. Transfer your music library (playlists, albums, artists, and songs) for free and securely.

[Go to the transfer tool](https://www.deezer.com/explore/fr/features/transfer-playlist/)
