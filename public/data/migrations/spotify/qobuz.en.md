## 1. Export Your Spotify Data
- Use a third-party service like [Soundiiz](https://soundiiz.com) or [TuneMyMusic](https://www.tunemymusic.com/) to export your playlists from Spotify.
- Log in with your Spotify account and select the playlists or tracks you want to transfer.

## 2. Import to Qobuz
- In the same tool, choose Qobuz as the destination platform.
- Log in to your Qobuz account.
- Follow the instructions to import your playlists and tracks.

## 3. Verify Your Library
- Check your Qobuz account to ensure all playlists and tracks have been transferred correctly.
- Some tracks may not be available on Qobuz due to catalog differences.
